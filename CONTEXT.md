# Scribe ATP — Domain Model

The Scribe ATP SDK reads author content from the AT Protocol and exposes it to consumer websites. Authors write in Scribe CMS; consumers display that content via this SDK.

## Language

**Author**:
The AT Protocol account that owns the content. Identified by a handle (e.g. `anthonycregan.dev`) or DID. Handles are resolved to DIDs before any fetch.
_Avoid_: User, owner, writer

**Site**:
A named publication belonging to an author — the top-level container for all their content. One author can have multiple sites. Stored as a `site.standard.publication` record on the author's PDS.
_Avoid_: Blog, publication (overloaded with the AT Protocol collection name)

**Publication URL**:
The spec-defined base URL of a Site (e.g. `"https://anthonycregan.co.uk"`). Stored in the `url` field of the `site.standard.publication` record. The canonical lookup key for a Site in the SDK — `fetchSite` and `fetchArticleBySlug` take this as their second parameter (`publicationUrl`). No trailing slash.
_Avoid_: Site URL (ambiguous), site slug (no longer the lookup key)

**Site Slug**:
A stable, human-readable identifier for a Site derived from its domain (`toSlug("norobots.blog")` → `"norobots-blog"`). Stored in `scribe.slug` of the publication record for CMS display and routing. Not used as a lookup key by the SDK — use Publication URL for that. Not the same as the Site's AT Protocol rkey.
_Avoid_: Site rkey, site key

**Group**:
A named, ordered collection of articles within a Site — a section or category. Has a `slug` (URL segment) and a `title`. Stored inside the Site record, not as a separate AT Protocol record.
_Avoid_: Category, section, collection

**Article**:
A single published piece of written content. Stored as a `site.standard.document` record on the author's PDS. The rkey is a TID generated by the PDS at publish time.
_Avoid_: Post, document (overloaded with the AT Protocol collection name)

**Article Slug**:
The human-readable URL segment for an article (e.g. `the-crows-of-shenton-way`). Stored in the `slug` field of the document record and in the `ArticleRef.slug` inside the Site record. Distinct from the article's AT Protocol rkey (a TID).
_Avoid_: Article rkey, article key

**Draft**:
An article that has not yet been placed in a named group. Stored as a `site.standard.document` record on the author's PDS and referenced in a Site's `ungroupedArticles`. `publishedAt` is absent.
_Avoid_: Unpublished article (overloaded — see Publication State)

**ArticleRef**:
A lightweight cached snapshot of an article's metadata embedded inside a Site record. Avoids N+1 fetches — the full article list can be rendered from the Site record alone. Contains `uri` (the full AT URI of the document, including TID rkey) and `slug` (the human-readable article slug).
_Avoid_: Article summary, article metadata

**TID (Timestamp Identifier)**:
A 13-character base32-sortable string encoding microseconds since epoch plus a clock ID. The AT Protocol rkey format required by the `site.standard.document` and `site.standard.publication` lexicons. Generated by the PDS when `createRecord` is called without an explicit rkey.
_Avoid_: Record key, rkey (too generic)

**rkey (Record Key)**:
The AT Protocol identifier for a specific record within a collection. For both `site.standard.document` and `site.standard.publication`, the rkey is always a TID generated by the PDS at creation time.

**Publication State**:
Every article is in one of two states: **Draft** (exists as a `site.standard.document` record referenced in a Site's `ungroupedArticles` — `publishedAt` is absent), or **Published** (referenced in a named Group within a Site — `publishedAt` is set).
_Avoid_: Status

**PDS (Personal Data Server)**:
The server hosting the author's AT Protocol data. Discovered via the author's DID document. All Scribe content lives on the author's PDS.

**Scribe Extension**:
Fields added to a `site.standard.*` record that are specific to Scribe and not part of the lexicon (e.g. `scribe.domain`, `scribe.basePath`, `scribe.groups`). The presence of a `scribe` field is used to distinguish Scribe-managed records from records created by other AT Protocol applications (e.g. OffPrint).
_Avoid_: Custom fields, extra fields
